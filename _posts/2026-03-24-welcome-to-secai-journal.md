## What is SecAI Journal?

This blog exists at the crossroads of two fields that are increasingly inseparable: **cybersecurity** and **artificial intelligence**.

As AI reshapes the threat landscape — from LLM-powered phishing to automated vulnerability discovery — understanding both domains together has never been more important.

## What to Expect

### CTF Writeups
Detailed solutions to Capture The Flag challenges with a focus on methodology, not just the flag. Understanding *why* an exploit works matters more than the exploit itself.

### AI-Powered Security Research
Exploring how large language models, machine learning, and automation can augment security workflows — for both attackers and defenders.

### Tool Reviews & Tutorials
Hands-on evaluations of security tools, from classic staples to emerging AI-assisted utilities.

### Threat Research
Analysis of malware, attack techniques, and real-world incidents through a technical lens.
